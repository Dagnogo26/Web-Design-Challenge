Humidity


As shown in the graph, there is no strong correlation between humidity and latitude. Being close to the equator on both end of the latitude spectrum does not seem to affect the humidity. For example, we noticed that 100% humidity can be experienced in both positive and negative latitude. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
